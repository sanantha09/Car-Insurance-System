Visualisation Descriptions

  • Bar Chart: Shows the distribution of driver ages. Each bar represents an age group with the height indicating the number of drivers.
  • Pie Chart: Displays the gender distribution of drivers. Each slice represents a gender proportion.
  • Radar Chart: Used for showing the marital status distribution by gender in a circular form, comparing multiple quantitative variables.
  • Scatter Chart: Plots each claim by age of the driver, showcasing patterns or correlations between age and number of claims.
  • Polar Area Chart: Represents types of cars in a circular format, ideal for comparing statistical data across categories.
